My name is Aska Djikia

I am a designer who specializes in lettering, coding, and illustration. I enjoy solving problems, learning about complex systems, and communicating information visually and accessibly. Ultimately, if a problem is interesting to me, my curiosity lies in finding sustainable, efficient, and accessible solutions, whatever the medium.

I was born in Germany, and since then I've lived in Russia, the US, Turkey, and now beautiful Canada is my home. It's been a blessing to learn so many cultures, but I find that it created in me a split desire for change and to settle in a home.

I have an ingrained love for the forest – as a Russian, mushroom picking is essentially a rite of passage — and I love to organize data to make it easier to read. I think my pattern recognition comes from staring closely at the natural world.

If I could do anything for the rest of my life, it would be to run an "ark" – help faciliate a space where nature grows as it wants to, in concert with local pollinators and animals. I would grow food, and wildflowers, and beekeep.
